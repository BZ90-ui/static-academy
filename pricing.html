Pricing questions

Quick answers about our one-time payment model.

Is this really a one-time payment with no recurring fees? +
Yes, 100%. You pay once and get lifetime access to all content in your plan — including every future update we release. There are no monthly fees, no annual renewals, and no hidden charges.
Can I upgrade from Pro to Elite later? +
Yes! You can upgrade at any time and you'll only pay the difference between the two plans. Your progress and account data are preserved when you upgrade.
Do you offer team or group discounts? +
Yes! For groups of 3+ people, we offer 15% off. For groups of 10+, we offer 25% off plus a dedicated onboarding session. Contact us at teams@staticacademy.com.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
What is the access policy? +
All sales are final. If you experience any technical issue accessing your content, our support team will resolve it at no charge.
