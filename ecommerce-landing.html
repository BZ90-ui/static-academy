FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Static Academy.

Do I need any experience to start? +
Absolutely not. Module 1 starts from scratch and assumes zero e-commerce knowledge. We take you from complete beginner to a fully operational store step by step. Many of our top students had never sold anything online before joining.
How long do I have access to the content? +
You get lifetime access — forever. This is a one-time payment, not a subscription. Free users keep access to Modules 1–2 forever. Pro and Elite members unlock their content permanently with a single purchase. No renewals, no expiry, no surprises.
What platforms do you cover? +
We cover Shopify (primary), WooCommerce, Amazon FBA, and TikTok Shop. The strategies taught are platform-agnostic where possible, so you can apply them anywhere. Module 3 has a dedicated section comparing all major platforms to help you choose the right one.
What is the purchase policy? +
All sales are final. As digital content is delivered immediately upon purchase, we do not offer refunds. If you experience any technical issue accessing your content, our support team will resolve it at no charge.
What's the difference between Pro and Elite? +
Pro ($247 one-time) gives you lifetime access to all 12 core modules, templates, tools, and a 1-on-1 coaching call. Elite ($497 one-time) adds 4 bonus modules, Appel vocal VIP mensuel (1h), weekly group coaching calls, a full business audit with Alex, and priority support. Both are one-time payments — no subscriptions.
How are the coaching calls structured? +
Pro members get one monthly 45-minute 1-on-1 call with a certified STATIC coach. Elite members get weekly group coaching calls (up to 90 min) plus their own monthly call with Alex directly. All calls are recorded and available in your dashboard.
